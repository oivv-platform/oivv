The Transparent Fashion Marketplace

The Fashion Industry is Broken.

Decades of opaque practices have created a system that is wasteful, expensive, and disconnected from its customers.

Opaque Pricing

Consumers pay 5-10x markups with no visibility into the true cost of production.

Overproduction Waste

Up to 40% of all fashion produced is never sold, leading to immense environmental waste.

High Return Rates

Poor online fit leads to ~25% return rates for apparel, creating massive logistical costs and waste.

Consumer Disconnect

Shoppers have no connection to the makers or the story behind their clothes.

Our Solution: An AI-Enhanced Ecosystem

MePont is not just an app. It's a multi-platform ecosystem built on three pillars, enhanced with AI to add value at every step.

Radical Transparency

A zero-inventory, crowdfunded model with a fully transparent price breakdown for every item.

Immersive Experience

A high-fidelity UE5 platform with physics-based virtual try-on that builds confidence and reduces returns.

Community & Connection

A platform that connects users directly to manufacturers and builds a community around shared values.
